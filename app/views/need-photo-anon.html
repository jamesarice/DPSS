Getting a photo code

In 4 steps

  1. Find a approved ID photo shop or photo booth. Use our handy look-up tool in the next step and plan your visit.
  2. Have your photo taken to passport standard.
  3. Choose to have your photo sent direct to DVLA and you'll be given a photo code.
  4. Come back to this website and sign in. Enter the 9 character code and your photo will be automatically added to your driving licence renewal.

Transferring a photograph from a registered photographer

In 4 steps

  1. Find a registered photographer near you. Use our handy look-up tool and plan your visit. (When you sign out from this website your application will be saved).
  2. Visit the photographer. Your photo will be taken to passport standard and uploaded to the Official Photo Repository by the photographer.
  3. Sign back in to this website. Enter the 8 digit code given to you by the photographer. Your photo will then be added automatically to your application.
  4. Complete the application. Submit it online.

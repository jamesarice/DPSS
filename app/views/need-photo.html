Getting a photo code

In 4 steps

  1. Find an approved photo shop or photo booth near you. Use our handy look-up tool and plan your visit. (When you sign out from this website your application will be saved).
  2. Visit the photo booth or photo shop and have your photo taken to passport standard. Your photo will be taken to passport standard and uploaded to the Official Photo Repository by the photographer.
  3. Choose to have your photo sent direct to DVLA and you'll be given a photo code. Enter the 8 digit code given to you by the photographer. Your photo will then be added automatically to your application.
  4. Come back to this website and sign in. Enter the 10 digit code and your photo will be automatically added to your driving licence renewal.
